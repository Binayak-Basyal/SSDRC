About SSDRC

SSDRC is a certified non-profit organization in Kathmandu, Nepal, dedicated to supporting children with Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD). Established in 2010, SSDRC provides special education, therapeutic services, and vocational training to underprivileged families. With the support of skilled professionals and dedicated volunteers, SSDRC aims to provide the best care and services to children with ASD, helping them lead fulfilling and productive lives.

Our Programs

Special Education

Combining ABA and Montessori methods, tailored to individual needs, to help children achieve academic and social milestones.

Rehabilitation

Developing life skills through therapy, dance, art, and yoga to help children with ASD integrate into society and lead independent lives.

Vocational Training

Providing employment opportunities and fostering independence by training young adults with ASD to take on meaningful roles in the workforce.

Family Support Services

Offering counseling, parenting workshops, and advocacy services to help families understand and support their children with ASD.

Community Awareness Programs

Organizing events and workshops to educate the general public about autism and how to create a more inclusive society for people with ASD.
